# OpenKids Africa 🌍

OpenKids Africa is a non-profit initiative working to expand access to digital skills and technology education for children in underserved communities across Africa.

---

## 🚀 What We Do

We provide free, accessible technology education using open digital resources and community-based programs.

Our work focuses on introducing children to:

* Coding and software development
* Digital literacy and problem-solving skills
* Emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence

We collaborate with schools and partners to bring these learning opportunities directly to communities where access is limited.

---

## 🌍 Why It Matters

Access to technology and digital skills remains deeply unequal across Africa, especially in rural communities.

Millions of children grow up without:

* Access to computers
* Reliable internet connectivity
* Exposure to digital learning opportunities

This gap limits future opportunities.

We believe every child deserves the chance to participate in the digital world — to learn, create, and shape the future.
